Destination

The World Awaits

InterContinental Dhaka is located in the prestigious downtown business district of Dhaka, which is the capital of Bangladesh. The hotel sits right next to the famous Ramna Park and is near Government Offices and other historic attractions.

DINING

Curated Culinary Experiences

Experience world class dining at InterContinental Dhaka. Enjoy international cuisines at Elements, a quick bite at Cafe Social, the juiciest steaks at The Amber Room, refreshing beverages at Opus and comfort food by the pool at Aqua Deck.
